Application Guide

A step-by-step companion for the Official 1003 Form.

Section 1: Borrower Info

1a. Personal Information
Accuracy Matters

Name: Must match your Driver's License exactly.

Marital Status: Only three choices: Married, Unmarried (Single/Divorced), or Separated.

Housing: If at current address < 2 years, you MUST complete the "Former Address" section.

1b. Employment (Current)
Self-Employed?

Ownership: Check "Self-Employed" if you own 25% or more of the business.

Income: Enter monthly income BEFORE taxes.

1c. Previous Employment
If Applicable

Only fill this out if you have been at your current job for less than 2 years.

1e. Income from Other Sources
If Applicable

Includes Alimony, Child Support, Disability, Pension, etc.

Tip: You do NOT have to list Alimony/Child Support unless you want it used to qualify for the loan.

Section 2: Assets & Liabilities

2a. Assets (Bank Accounts)

List Checking, Savings, and Money Market accounts.

Cash: Do not list physical cash ("mattress money").

2b. Other Assets

Retirement (401k/IRA), Stocks, Cryptocurrency, or Proceeds from a Sale.

2c. Liabilities (Debts)

We usually auto-populate this from your credit report. Only add debts that might not show up (like private loans).

Section 3: Real Estate

3a. Property You Own

If you don't own any real estate, check "Does Not Apply".

If you DO own: List all properties (including the one you are selling, if any).

3b. Mortgage Loans
If Applicable

List the mortgages attached to the properties in 3a.

3c. Title Information
If Applicable

If you have owned a home in the last 3 years, answer these questions about how you held the title (Sole vs Joint).

Section 4: Loan & Property

4a. Loan Purpose

Purchase: Buying a home.

Refinance: No Cash Out vs. Cash Out.

4b. Other New Loans
If Applicable

Are you taking out a second mortgage (like a HELOC) on this property at the same time as this loan?

4c. Rental Income
If Applicable

Complete this if the property you are buying is a 2-4 unit Investment Property where you expect rental income.

4d. Gifts & Grants

If using a Gift for down payment, list it here. You will need a signed "Gift Letter" later.

Section 5: Declarations

5a. Property & Funding Questions

These questions ask about the property itself and where your money is coming from.

Important: You must disclose if any part of your down payment is borrowed.

5b. Financial History

These questions cover your financial past, including Bankruptcy, Foreclosure, Lawsuits, and Co-Signed debts.

Section 6: Agreements

6. Acknowledgments

You are certifying that the info is true. Lying on a 1003 is mortgage fraud.

You also authorize us to order your credit report/appraisal.

Section 7: Military

7. Military Service

Check "Yes" if you are a Veteran, Active Duty, or Surviving Spouse.

This checks eligibility for VA Loans ($0 Down).

Section 8: Demographics

8. HMDA Info

You may check "I do not wish to provide this information" for Race, Sex, and Ethnicity.
